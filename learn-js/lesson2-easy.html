Lesson 2: What is a Variable? 📦

1 Real Life Example

You know how you label your school folders?

One says "Math", another says "Science", another says "English".

Each folder holds different stuff inside it.

A variable is the same thing! It's a name that holds something inside.

📁 myName
"Jiten"
📁 myAge
10
📁 myPet
"Dog"

2 How to Make One

To make a variable, you write just one line:

let myName = "Jiten";

Let's break it down word by word:

let 💬 "Hey computer, make me a new folder!"
myName 💬 "Write this label on the folder"
= 💬 "Put this thing inside it"
"Jiten" 💬 "This is what goes inside!"

A variable = a labeled folder that holds something inside.
