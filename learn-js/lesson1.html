Lesson 1: Hello, World! 🎉

What you'll learn: How to make the computer show messages on the screen using console.log()

Step 1: Say Hello!

Click the button below to run your first line of code:

console.log("Hello, World!");

Step 2: Say More Things!

Now let's print multiple lines. Click below:

console.log("My name is Jiten!");
console.log("I am 10 years old!");
console.log("I am learning JavaScript!");

Step 3: Your Turn! 🎨

Type your own message below and click Run! Change the text inside the quotes to say anything you want.


What You Learned Today 🎓

console.log() tells the computer to show a message.

Text must go inside " " (quotation marks).

Every line of code ends with a ; (semicolon) - it's like a period at the end of a sentence!
