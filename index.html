How to Play

PC Controls

Arrow Keys / WASD - Move   Space - Jump   Mouse - Look

Left Click - Attack   Shift+Click - Break blocks

Right Click - Place block / Use food & potions

E - Inventory   1-0 - Hotbar   Scroll - Cycle slots

ESC - Pause   Click to lock mouse

iPad / iPhone Controls

Left joystick - Move around

Swipe right side of screen - Look around

ATK - Attack enemies   JMP - Jump

BRK - Break blocks   USE - Place block / Use item

INV - Open inventory   TEAM - Show teammate arrows

| | (top of screen) - Pause game

Game Modes

Single Player (Survival)

Fight waves of monsters that get harder each round. Every 3 waves a boss spawns! Kill enemies to earn XP and collect loot drops. Choose Easy, Medium, or Hard difficulty - all start with 200 HP. Survive as long as you can!

PvP Arena

Battle AI fighters in an arena. New waves of fighters spawn every 15 seconds. Kill them for XP. No monsters spawn - just you vs fighters!

CTF (Capture the Flag)

You are on the Blue team with 8 blue bots as allies. Walk near the Red flag to grab it automatically, then bring it back to your Blue base to score! 8 red bots guard their flag. Press TEAM to see arrows pointing to teammates, the red flag, and your blue base with distances. First team to capture wins!

Online Multiplayer

Host a game to get a 6-digit room code. Share the code with friends so they can join. The host picks the mode (PvP or CTF). You get a 5-weapon loadout for online play. All players share the same world!

Quick Match

Instantly play with anyone online! Tap Find Match and the game searches for other players. The first player becomes the host automatically, others connect within seconds. The game mode is picked randomly (PvP or CTF). You get a 5-weapon loadout just like Online Multiplayer. No room codes needed - just tap and play!

Shop & Loadout

Earn XP from kills to buy new characters and weapons in the Shop. Characters must be unlocked in order (cheapest first). Each character has a unique bonus (speed, HP, damage, regen, range, or a combo). Weapons range from free (Knife, Pistol, Bow) to legendary tier. Enchant weapons up to +5 for bonus damage!

Inventory

• Press E (PC) or INV (mobile) to open your inventory

• Click/tap items to move them between slots

• Each slot holds up to 99 of the same item

• Use 1-0 keys or scroll wheel to select hotbar slots

• Select food/potions in hotbar, then right-click (PC) or USE (mobile) to consume

• Food heals HP instantly, potions give temporary buffs

• Buy food & potions once in the Item Shop - then pick them in your loadout before each game (10 food, 5 potions)

Minimap

The minimap is in the top-right corner of the screen. It shows:

• Yellow dot = You

• White dots = Blue teammates (CTF)

• Red dots = Enemies

• Blue square = Blue base (CTF)

• Red square = Red flag (CTF)

• Green = Grass/terrain   Blue = Water   Brown = Dirt

Tips

• All difficulties start with 200 HP

• Stay out of water - you drown after 3 seconds!

• HP regens automatically over time

• Killing enemies drops loot cubes - walk over them to collect

• Use the Loadout screen to equip owned weapons & characters

• Mini Admin (password protected) lets you make announcements & gift XP

• Your progress syncs across devices via cloud save
