How to Play

PC Controls

Arrow Keys / WASD - Move   Space - Jump   Mouse - Look

Left Click - Attack   Shift+Click - Break blocks

Right Click - Place block / Use food & potions

E - Inventory   V - Toggle 3rd person camera

1-0 - Hotbar   Scroll - Cycle slots

ESC - Pause   Click to lock mouse

iPad / iPhone Controls

Left joystick - Move around

Swipe right side of screen - Look around

ATK - Attack enemies   JMP - Jump

BRK - Break blocks   USE - Place block / Use item

INV - Open inventory   TEAM - Show teammate arrows   👁 - Toggle 3rd person

| | (top of screen) - Pause game

Game Modes

Single Player (Survival)

Fight waves of monsters that get harder each round. Every 3 waves a boss spawns! Kill enemies to earn XP and collect loot drops. Choose Easy, Medium, or Hard difficulty. Survive as long as you can!

PvP Arena

Offline: Battle AI fighters. Online: Fight real players! No bots - just you vs other players.

CTF (Capture the Flag)

You are on the Blue team with 8 blue bots. Grab the Red flag and bring it back to your Blue base to score! Press TEAM to see arrows to teammates, flag, and base. Blue base gives 3x regen and 2x HP recovery!

Online Multiplayer

Host a game to get a 6-digit room code. Share it so friends can join. The host picks PvP or CTF. You get a 5-weapon loadout for online play.

Quick Match

Instantly play with anyone online! Tap Find Match - the game auto-connects you with other players. Mode is random (PvP or CTF). No room codes needed!

Shop & Loadout

Earn XP from kills to buy characters, weapons, food & potions. Items unlock in order (cheapest first). Each character has a unique bonus. Weapons range from free (Stick, Rock) to Supreme (1M damage!). Food ranges from 1 HP (Blueberry) to 1000 HP (Ultima Feast). Potions go from basic to God tier (10x buffs!). Enchant weapons up to +5.

For online games you pick a loadout of 5 weapons, 1 food (x10), and 1 potion (x5). Offline games give you 3 weapons.

Special Characters: The Creator (+99900% everything), Teleporter (same stats + tap minimap to teleport anywhere!)

Inventory

• Press E (PC) or INV (mobile) to open inventory

• Click/tap items to move between slots

• Each slot holds up to 99 of the same item

• Use 1-0 keys or scroll wheel to select hotbar

• Right-click (PC) or USE (mobile) to consume food/potions

• Food heals HP instantly, potions give temporary buffs

Potion Effects

• Heal - Instantly restore HP

• Speed - Move faster for a duration

• Strength - Deal more damage

• Shield - Take less damage

• Invincibility (⭐) - Block ALL damage for 10 seconds!

Camera

• Press V (PC) or 👁 (mobile) to toggle 3rd person

• See your character model and held weapon from behind

• First-person shows your weapon at bottom-right

Minimap

Top-right corner of screen (160x160):

• Yellow = You   White = Teammates   Red = Enemies

• Cyan square (white border) = Blue base   Red square = Red flag

• Green = Grass   Blue = Water   Brown = Dirt

• Teleporter character: Tap/click the minimap to instantly teleport there!

Seasons & Weather

The world changes with real-life seasons based on your actual location!

• 🌸 Spring (Mar-May) - Bright green, flowers blooming

• ☀️ Summer (Jun-Aug) - Deep green, vivid skies

• 🍂 Autumn (Sep-Nov) - Golden/brown leaves

• ❄️ Winter (Dec-Feb) - Snow-covered ground, falling snow

• 🌴 Tropical areas get Rainy/Dry seasons instead of snow

• 🥶 Cold areas get harsh winters and snow in autumn

• 🌏 Southern hemisphere seasons are flipped!

• 🌧️ Rain falls based on real-world climate of your location (monsoons in tropics, spring showers in temperate zones)

• Set your location in Settings to get accurate weather!

Special Days

• ⚔️ Mar 11 - Power Day! x2 damage for everyone

• 💨 Jun 3 - Speed Day! x2 speed for everyone

• 🎂 Oct 9 - Birthday! 400 HP for everyone

Tips

• Stay out of water - you drown after 3 seconds!

• HP regens automatically over time

• Killing enemies drops glowing loot - walk over to collect

• In CTF, stand near your Blue base for boosted healing

• Your progress syncs across devices via cloud save

• Usernames are case-insensitive (Player1 = player1)

• Grass blades and flowers grow on terrain for a natural look

• Enemies have glowing eyes - look for them in the dark!
