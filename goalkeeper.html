How to Play

  • Arrow Keys — Move the goalkeeper around the field
  • Tap — Tap anywhere on the field to move (iPad/phone)
  • Block the ball — Stop the soccer ball from going in the goal
  • Kick it away — Move into the ball to kick it in your direction
  • 3 Hearts — You lose a heart each time the ball enters the goal
  • Space — Pause the game between balls
  • Enter — Start the game
  • The ball gets faster and intervals get shorter as you score!
